About

XR hardware veteran with a rare ability to operate across the full product stack — from component sourcing and ODM management to R&D liaison and global deployment. I introduced Acer to VR, co-managed the world's largest VR arcade center in Dubai, and managed the hardware supply chain for Lynx Mixed Reality from first production through the R1 lifecycle.

At Acer, I also led exploratory R&D around emerging sensors — depth cameras, motion sensors, and biometrics — developing prototypes that defined new product usages and interactions. This hands-on innovation work translated directly into shipped consumer products, including an award-winning laptop (Red Dot 2015) and a convertible PC that earned the Innovation Design Award 2014, bridging lab experimentation with market-ready hardware.

Alongside my tech career, I've built and scaled four independent businesses in Taipei — including a cocktail bar, a restaurant, and a nightlife venue — developing sharp instincts for brand, operations, and growth.

I operate fluently in French, English, and Mandarin, bridging Western strategy with Asian execution across supply chains and partnerships.
